ABOUT US

RAFT, or "Randomly Assign/Find Tables", is a website that simplifies the process of discovering new restaurants. Whether you're vacationing, on a business trip, or just want to explore new local places, your next meal is only one click away.

RAFT is dedicated to the adventurous foodies, broke college students, and indecisive couples of this world. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do!
